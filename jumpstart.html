BRAND OVERVIEW


Jumpstart is a civic accessibility brand that helps people connect with government services through clear design, human guidance, and innovative touchpoints. Built on a foundation of trust, empathy, and action — from virtual gateways to real-world pop-ups — we make essential resources easier to understand and access.

Who?

A civic brand built on trust, clarity, and empathy. Jumpstart acts as a guide that is approachable, knowledgeable, and genuinely invested in helping people navigate complex systems with confidence.

What?

A bridge between people and public services, designed for real life. Through intuitive tools, human connection, and thoughtful design, Jumpstart transforms bureaucracy into accessibility.

Why?

Jumpstart exists to empower communities, making it easier for everyone to understand, reach, and benefit from the services that exist to support them.
