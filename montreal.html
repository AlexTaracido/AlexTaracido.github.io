BRAND OVERVIEW


Montréal, nous t’invitons is an advertising campaign that celebrates Montréal’s bilingual spirit and everyday charm. Building on a foundation of inclusivity, connection, and local life — from art and design to transit, parks, and people. Our identity is warm and adaptable, representing Montreal as a city welcoming you to make it your own.

Who?

Montréal, nous t’invitons is an advertising campaign that celebrates Montréal’s bilingual spirit and everyday charm. Building on a foundation of inclusivity, connection, and local life — from art and design to transit, parks, and people. Our identity is warm and adaptable, representing Montreal as a city welcoming you to make it your own.

What?

A modern expression of Montréal through design, language, and lived experience. Every touchpoint highlights what makes the city livable, lovable, and unmistakably its own.

Why?

To show why Montréal stands apart — a city that welcomes you in, moves at your pace, and makes belonging feel effortless.
