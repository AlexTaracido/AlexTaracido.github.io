BRAND OVERVIEW


We champion local culture, creative curation, and sustainable practices. By combining whimsical discovery with purposeful impact, Pigeon’s Nest creates experiences that are personal, memorable, and meaningful.

Who?

Pigeon’s Nest is a trinket curation service that blends personalization, sustainability, and community support to create a one-of-a-kind shopping experience.

What?

We match customers with their personal aesthetic through a style quiz, then send local curators to handpick locally thrifted treasures just for them. These curated finds are delivered via autonomous vehicles, and a portion of proceeds supports pigeon advocacy.

Why?

In a world of mass production and fast fashion, meaningful objects and personal touches are being lost. Pigeon’s Nest gives new life to forgotten treasures, supports small businesses, and helps rewrite the narrative on one of the most misunderstood creatures—pigeons. Customers don’t just shop; they connect with history, community, and a movement toward sustainable, thoughtful consumption.
